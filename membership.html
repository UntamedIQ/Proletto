Frequently Asked Questions

Can I cancel my subscription at any time?

Yes, you can cancel your subscription at any time. If you cancel, you'll continue to have access to Supporter features until the end of your current billing period.

How does the 14-day free trial work?

You'll get full access to all Supporter features for 14 days without being charged. After the trial period, you'll be billed automatically unless you cancel before the trial ends.

Which state engines are available?

We currently offer engines for New York, California, Texas, Illinois, Massachusetts, Florida, Washington, Oregon, Colorado, and Pennsylvania. We're constantly adding more states based on member feedback.

Will the price increase in the future?

Beta Supporters are guaranteed the $5/month price for as long as they maintain their subscription, even if we raise prices in the future.

When will the Agent tier be available?

We're planning to launch the Agent tier in Q3 2025. Join the waitlist to be notified as soon as it's available and get early access.
