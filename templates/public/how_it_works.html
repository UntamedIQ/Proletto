How Proletto Works

Proletto is designed to transform how artists discover and secure opportunities through AI-powered matching, portfolio optimization, and application assistance.

01

AI-Powered Opportunity Matching

Our specialized engines continuously collect opportunities from across the art world, with geographic specialization for the top art states.

  • Personalized recommendations based on your portfolio
  • Access to grants, residencies, exhibitions, and commissions
  • Receive notifications when relevant opportunities are found
  • Save favorites to apply later

Essentials Tier: Access to basic opportunity database

Insider Tier: Advanced matching + priority alerts

Gallery Tier: Complete access to all specialized engines

02

Portfolio Optimization

Our AI analyzes your portfolio and provides actionable insights to increase your chances of success with specific opportunity types.

  • Get personalized recommendations for improvements
  • Optimize your portfolio for specific opportunities
  • Understand what grant reviewers and curators look for
  • Track your portfolio's performance over time

Essentials Tier: Basic portfolio analysis

Insider Tier: Detailed optimization recommendations

Gallery Tier: Advanced analytics + benchmark comparisons

03

Application Assistance

Save time and improve your applications with our AI-powered submission tools that help you complete applications quickly and effectively.

  • Auto-populate application forms with your profile information
  • Get suggestions for artist statements and proposal text
  • Track application status and deadlines
  • Receive reminders for upcoming deadlines

Essentials Tier: Basic application tracking

Insider Tier: AI-assisted application drafting

Gallery Tier: Premium auto-submission features

04

Collaborative Workspace

Work seamlessly with clients, galleries, and collaborators in a dedicated workspace designed for visual artists.

  • Share and organize your portfolio with clients
  • Manage projects and track deliverables
  • Securely communicate and share files
  • Create professional proposals and invoices

Insider Tier: Basic workspace features

Gallery Tier: Advanced client management tools
